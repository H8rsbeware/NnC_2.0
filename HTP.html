NnC/HTP
  • Play
  • HTP
  • LTA
  • The aim is to get 3 in a row on a 3x3 board
  • Each player places their marker (X or O) and then the other person places
  • You cant place a piece on an already filled spot
  • The game can be won horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
  • If all squares are filled with no winner, then its a tie
  • You can play against a computer, or a friend
