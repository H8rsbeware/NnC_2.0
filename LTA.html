What is Minimax

Minimax is a decision rule and recursive algorithm used in artificial intelligence, it works out the best move on each turn, and the best move it can play in response, allowing it to “think ahead” when choosing a placement. The algorithm works on the idea of minimising lose, and can be illustrated by a tree with nodes containing numbers; player alternate, with one tying to end with the highest number possible and the other, the lowest. Working backwards, a branch can be given a weight based on the move that will be taken so that the first players move can be calculated so they are guaranteed a win.

With each recursion of the algorithm, the weight given is made smaller, this is to ensure that the AI wins in as few moves as possible, rather than taking a longer route for the same outcome. The algorithms difficulty can be ajusted by changing the max depth of the algorithm as it cant predict the players future moves when making its decision.
